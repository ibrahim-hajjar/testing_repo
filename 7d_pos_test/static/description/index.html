FEATURES

Generates the POS Order even if the product is unavailable at the POS location

Captures the POS sale order and accepts payment against it

Auto-Creates a purchase order / sales order within your inter-company procurement source

Auto-Creates the transfer operations from your inter-company source to your POS location

HOW IT WORKS

Configuration

1- On the Product Card, under Inventory tab; select checkbox [Make to Order POS]

2- On the Product Card, under the Purchase tab; assign an inter-company vendor and cost-price

3- In the POS Session Configuration, select the checkbox [Make to Order POS]

Operations

1- When a POS Order is created and the product is unavailable at that time; our module will auto-create a purchase order to the inter-company source and a sales order is auto-created thereafter. Furthermore, a transfer will be auto-created between the two companies

2- You can validate the POS sale order the moment you validate the stock transfer
