FEATURES

Adds a simple checkbox to the Native Feature of Odoo's Reordering Rules

Manages the cumulative demand-supply of inventory across your Warehouses and Sales Locations

Auto-Creates RFQs demanding the Maximum Quantity when your cumulative inventory drops below its Minimum Quantity

HOW IT WORKS

Configure the Rule:

1- Define the Minimum and Maximum Quantity of each SKU (Product) to meet the cumulative inventory demands of the warehouses and all sales locations

2- Tick the Checkbox [Based on Stock_Type POS & WH

3- Select the destination Warehouse location

4- Define the schedule you need this module to read the inventory levels

5- Ensure every SKU has its Vendor and Cost Price pre-defined

Configure the Scheduler

Define the schedule you need the App to create automated RFQs in the [Run Scheduler] (accessed in debug mode)

Rule in Effect:

As the cumulative stock quantity (across all locations) of an SKU drops below its Minimums, a RFQ will be autogenerated in the Purchase app for the deficit quantity to reach that SKUâ€™s Maximums

Conclude the Process

All you need to do is Convert that RFQ to a Purchase Order

EXAMPLE

Product X; Minimum 10 / Maximum 25 / Current Inventory 9 Our Redefined Reordering Rules will create a RFQ for 16 ( where; 16 + 9 = 25 ) Maximum Here, 9 has dropped below the minimum of 10 and here 16 is the deficit to reach its maximum of 25
